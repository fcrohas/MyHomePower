How It Works

From data collection to appliance identification in 4 simple steps

1

Connect to Home Assistant

Connect your Home Assistant instance to access real-time and historical power consumption data from your smart meter or power sensors.

2

Tag Your Data

Manually label time periods when specific appliances are running to create training data for the deep learning models.

3

Train Models

Use the ML Trainer to build custom models for each appliance, or import pre-trained models from the community library.

4

Analyze & Monitor

Run predictions to disaggregate total power into individual appliances, detect anomalies, and export data to Home Assistant Energy Dashboard.
